Jane Pyley
Jane Pyley is a Human who is an adept of the Brotherhood. She was born to a middle class family in Wisfield, later moving to Whitford to attend the famous Whitfold Military Academy. After finishing the period of study she served in the army as a lesser officer. Later she was dismissed, as relayed by her : "For inorderly conduct, or whatever... it was long ago". Known in the Brotherhood for violent tendencies and unusual beliefs.
