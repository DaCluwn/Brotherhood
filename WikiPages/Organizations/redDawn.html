Red Dawn
A Mercenary company operating mostly in West District
Consists of 1-3 important members. None of whom are known to the Brotherhood
And 10-20 unimportant ones.
Their base of operations is located in West District at Mastiff's Avenue. They conduct their business in the Drunken Curr inn and tavern. Being a public place there are no restrictions on if you can enter.
Their main source of income is from work as hired swords.
their relations with other organizations are unknown.
