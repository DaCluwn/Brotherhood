Greybeard Trading Company
A trade conclave mostly operating in The Docks
Consists of 1-5 important members. None of whom are known to the Brotherhood
And 10-20 unimportant ones.
Their base of operations is located in The Docks at the Moor Close. It is a small, but decently constructed and decorated guild hall. Access to their property is public for conducting business, however there are restricted areas.
Their main source of income is trade, in particulat naval trade with island territories of Principality.
their relations with other organizations are unknown.
