South Town
Located in the city of Lifford, South Town is a lively district that emanates a distinct charm and rich history. Characterized by its winding streets and well-preserved architecture. The focal point of South Town is its bustling market square, where merchants gather to sell their wares. Here, visitors can find a vibrant array textiles, and exotic goods, creating a feast for the senses and an opportunity to procure rare treasures. As one walks through the cobbled streets, they will notice the well-constructed timber-framed buildings that line the district. Many of these buildings have been converted into shops, taverns and inns. South Town is home to several historic landmarks that offer deeper insights into the city's past. The towering Lifford Castle stands commanding attention with its robust walls and towers. In South Town festivals and cultural events celebrating the city's heritage held regularly
