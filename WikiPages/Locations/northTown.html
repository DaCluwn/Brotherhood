North Town
North Town is one of the districts within the city of Lifford. As one steps into Northtown, they are greeted by wide cobblestone streets that This district is known as a hub for commerce and craft, with numerous guild halls located in the district. Here, merchants and craftsmen gather to showcase their wares, the atmosphere is filled with the buzz of haggling customers. In the heart of Northtown, there is a square where locals and visitors often gather. The square is dominated by a towering stone fountain, adorned with intricate carvings representing mythical creatures and historical figures. Surrounding the fountain, benches provide a resting place for weary travelers, while nearby taverns and eateries offer a chance for refreshment and camaraderie. Northtown also serves as an essential link between the city's fortified walls and the bustling harbor. As a result, it is not uncommon to witness a steady stream of porters, wagons and sailors passing through the district.
