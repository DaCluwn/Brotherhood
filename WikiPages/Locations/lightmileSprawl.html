Lightmile Sprawl
Lightmile Sprawl is a decaying slum district located outside the southern gates of the city. This neglected corner of the city is home to a diverse community, where hustle and bustle of daily life intertwines with the hardships and struggles of its inhabitants. Lightmile Sprawl. The district bears the marks of a place left behind by progress and development. Dilapidated buildings stand alongside each other, their facades painted with graffiti and fading murals. The streets of Lightmile Sprawl are a labyrinthine network, winding around haphazardly, leading to hidden market stalls, communal squares, and dimly lit taverns. Much like Elmwood sprawl the Lightmile Sprawl is divided into Southern and northern portions
